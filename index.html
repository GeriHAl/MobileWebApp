NYSL

Our purpose is to utilize NYSL to enhance positive human development. Our mission is to provide access to NYSL for all our Players. Yout development is the core of our mission, and the heart of our passion. NYSL set about implementing a holistic developmental platform integrating world-class player training and sport science for the youth soccer players of the NYSL region. NYSL is the renowned soccer platform providing an opportunity for the youngest players to develop a lifelong passion for the game and elite academy-aged players, on the cusp of professional stardom, the opportunity to pursue excellence in an enriching and nurturing environment.
Our vision is a state in which every players has access to a version of the world's beautiful game.
We shall: Promote a healthy, safe and fun playing environment. Ensure a dependable, accountable, and caring organization. Build health and character through a focus on fair play and good sportsmanship. Promote integrity, high ethics and moral standards. Provide inclusive opportunities for everyone to participate. Encourage teamwork, leadership, respect and family values!
